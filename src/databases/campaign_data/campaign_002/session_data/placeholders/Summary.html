I. Session Overview

  • Session Number: 1
  • In-Game Date/Time: Day 4 of travel, afternoon.
  • Location(s) Explored: Road to Drellin's Ferry, Abandoned Farmhouse, Drellin's Ferry (Tollhouse, Shrine of Pelor, Sertieren's Mansion)
  • The Big Headline: The party survived a hobgoblin ambush and arrived at Drellin's Ferry, where they learned of the Wyrmlord Koth and his "Day of Ruin" plan while beginning investigations into local threats.

II. Narrative Summary

Travel and Ambush

  • Party traveling toward Drellin’s Ferry through subtropical forest.
  • Encountered abandoned farmhouse in shallow dell; Bonnie identifies it as potential ambush site.
  • Ambushed by a group of hobgoblins:
    • Terrain and fatigue make battle challenging.
    • Party coordinates attacks: Norr (lightning), Soshi (fire), Kaedin (echo strike), Olek (melee), Bonnie (dagger).
    • Enemy captain killed; other enemies retreat.
  • Captured one hobgoblin survivor:
    • Interrogation initially fails (hobgoblin silent).
    • Olek casts Zone of Truth; hobgoblin finally agrees to answer in exchange for promised swift death.
    • Answers:
      • Warlord: Wyrmlord Koth, a captain gathering tribes for “Day of Ruin.”
      • Plans: vague, details unclear.
      • Location: unknown/refused.
    • Hobgoblin released and killed by Olek per promise.
  • Party burns three dead travelers’ bodies to prevent misuse, then carefully extinguishes fire.

Encounter with Civilians

  • Farmer and two sons walk toward the farmhouse:
    • Olek tries to warn them; they panic at his appearance.
    • Bonnie telepathically frightens them (“Boo”), causing them to flee.
    • Bonnie collects supplies left behind; Olek returns most items except spoiled wine.

Arrival at Drellin’s Ferry

  • Town description: along river, ferryboats replace missing bridge, orchards, fields, five watchtowers.
  • Met town guards led by Sergeant Hersk:
    • Briefed on local dangers (Witchwood, goblins, orcs, undead rumors).
    • Requested directions: lodging, Sorana, other landmarks.
  • Party splits:
    • Olek + Bonnie → Tollhouse (contact Sorana, report ambush).
    • Norr + Soshi → Shrine of Pelor (speak with Brother Denny and druid Avartel).
    • Kaedin → Mansion of wizard Sertieren (seek information on Talos and magic).

Olek & Bonnie at Tollhouse

  • Encounter Sorana:
    • Bonnie delivers artifact from Rilon Paln.
    • Sorana identifies one item: Dead Man’s Boots, last held by necromancer Nels Huto, heading into Witchwood.
  • Discuss town threats with Norro (bald man in rich garb):
    • Raids are ongoing; “Day of Ruin” not recognized.
    • Base likely deep in Witchwood.
    • Offer: stop raiders for reward.
  • Olek mounts steed with Bonnie to reunite with party.

Norr & Soshi at Shrine of Pelor

  • Purchase offerings for the shrine (greens and fruits for 5 silver each).
  • Meet Brother Denny and Avartel:
    • Avartel provides information on Witchwood and Wrath Keep (fortress, cursed land, skeletons).
    • Brother Denny accepts offerings; tries to sell wares.
  • Depart shrine for meeting point.

Kaedin at Sertieren Mansion

  • Encounters magical fountain (speaks) and halfling servant.
  • Asks wizard Sertieren about Talos, disappearances, and magical phenomena:
    • Mentions Chronomancers of Krynn (rumors of deaths by dragons).
    • Notes magical nexus in Witchwood; area dangerous.
    • Comments on city defenses and local threats.
  • Kaedin departs mansion; heads to meeting point.
