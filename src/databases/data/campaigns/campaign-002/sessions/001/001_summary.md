<h3>II. Narrative Summary</h3>
<p><strong>Travel and Ambush</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>Party traveling toward Drellin&rsquo;s Ferry through subtropical forest.</li>
	<li>Encountered abandoned farmhouse in shallow dell; Bonnie identifies it as potential ambush site.</li>
	<li>Ambushed by a group of hobgoblins:</li>
	<ul>
		<li>Terrain and fatigue make battle challenging.</li>
		<li>
			Party coordinates attacks: Norr (lightning), Soshi (fire), Kaedin (echo strike), Olek (melee), Bonnie (dagger).
		</li>
		<li>Enemy captain killed; other enemies retreat.</li>
	</ul>
	<li>Captured one hobgoblin survivor:</li>
	<ul>
		<li>Interrogation initially fails (hobgoblin silent).</li>
		<li>Olek casts Zone of Truth; hobgoblin finally agrees to answer in exchange for promised swift death.</li>
		<li>Answers:</li>
		<ul>
			<li>Warlord: Wyrmlord Koth, a captain gathering tribes for &ldquo;Day of Ruin.&rdquo;</li>
			<li>Plans: vague, details unclear.</li>
			<li>Location: unknown/refused.</li>
		</ul>
		<li>Hobgoblin released and killed by Olek per promise.</li>
	</ul>
	<li>Party burns three dead travelers&rsquo; bodies to prevent misuse, then carefully extinguishes fire.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Encounter with Civilians</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>Farmer and two sons walk toward the farmhouse:</li>
	<ul>
		<li>Olek tries to warn them; they panic at his appearance.</li>
		<li>Bonnie telepathically frightens them (&ldquo;Boo&rdquo;), causing them to flee.</li>
		<li>Bonnie collects supplies left behind; Olek returns most items except spoiled wine.</li>
	</ul>
</ul>
<p><strong>Arrival at Drellin&rsquo;s Ferry</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>Town description: along river, ferryboats replace missing bridge, orchards, fields, five watchtowers.</li>
	<li>Met town guards led by Sergeant Hersk:</li>
	<ul>
		<li>Briefed on local dangers (Witchwood, goblins, orcs, undead rumors).</li>
		<li>Requested directions: lodging, Soranna, other landmarks.</li>
	</ul>
	<li>Party splits:</li>
	<ul>
		<li>Olek + Bonnie &rarr; Tollhouse (contact Soranna, report ambush).</li>
		<li>Norr + Soshi &rarr; Shrine of Pelor (speak with Brother Denny and druid Avartel).</li>
		<li>Kaedin &rarr; Mansion of wizard Sertieren (seek information on Talos and magic).</li>
	</ul>
</ul>
<p><strong>Olek &amp; Bonnie at Tollhouse</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>Encounter Soranna:</li>
	<ul>
		<li>Bonnie delivers artifact from Rilon Paln.</li>
		<li>
			Soranna identifies one item: Dead Man&rsquo;s Boots, last held by necromancer Nels Huto, heading into Witchwood.
		</li>
	</ul>
	<li>Discuss town threats with Norro (bald man in rich garb):</li>
	<ul>
		<li>Raids are ongoing; &ldquo;Day of Ruin&rdquo; not recognized.</li>
		<li>Base likely deep in Witchwood.</li>
		<li>Offer: stop raiders for reward.</li>
	</ul>
	<li>Olek mounts steed with Bonnie to reunite with party.</li>
</ul>
